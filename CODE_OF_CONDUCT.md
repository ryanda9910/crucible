# Code of Conduct

## Our Standards

- Be specific and constructive in feedback
- Focus on the work, not the person
- Respect that different use cases have different right answers
- Document decisions, don't just make them

## Not acceptable

- Harassment, personal attacks, or dismissive language
- Spam PRs or issues without substance
- Deliberately breaking others' contributions

## Enforcement

Violations reported to [ryandaaldo@gmail.com](mailto:ryandaaldo@gmail.com) will be reviewed and actioned within 7 days.

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
